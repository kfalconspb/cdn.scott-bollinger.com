VeeamON 2018 Conference Alright, Who's Going?

What?

VeeamON is the annual conference for Veeam Customers and Partners. Two and a half days of General Sessions, Keynotes, over 60 breakout sessions, talking to Veeam Experts, getting certified, and exploring the newest technologies and features with hands on labs.

Where?

At McCormick Place conference center in Chicago, IL 2301 S. King Dr, Chicago, IL 60616.

The two hotels listed below are close to the venue, but of course since it's Chicago, there's no shortage of great hotels and restaurants.

When?

Only 59 days left, until the conference! VeeamON is being held on May 14th - 16th, 2018.
