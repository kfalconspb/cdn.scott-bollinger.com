Last February, I attended the RSA Conference and BSides San Francisco and it was quite a first time experience for both.

RSA was HUGE

I've been to industry conferences over the years. Conferences like VMworld and Synergy, but RSA was freaking huge.  It was their 25th anniversary and they had a record number of more than 40,000 attendees. By the way the conference was packed. Not stand in lines forever Def Con packed, but a massive amount of people. I'm glad to hear that for 2017 they are expanding to not only include the Moscone Center but the Marriott Marquis too.
