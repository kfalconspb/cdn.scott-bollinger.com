RSAC 2018 OnDemand Videos

As you know, a few of us were away at the RSA Conference last week.

I just wanted to bring your attention that, the OnDemand Videos of the conference are available online.

I've also listed below, two of my favorites off the list.

The OnDemand page can be found at https://www.rsaconference.com/events/us18/rsac-ondemand/watch You may Sign up, and recieve the same code sent to everyone: ONDEMANDUS2018 <- Note all UpperCase

My Picks

The first suggestion is, Securing Diversity - Diversity in Cybersecurity - Changing the Conversation Professor Kim Jones, Director, Cybersecurity Education Consortium, Arizona State University https://www.rsaconference.com/events/us18/rsac-ondemand/securing-diversity-diversity-in-cybersecurity

I agree with Mr. Jones, that these conversations need to be brought to the big room. But also back to our own teams. We are a great example or the kind of success, diversity can provide. As our team has grown, each of us has added, their unique background, and ideas to the team. We are structured in such a way, that as you voice your opinions and ideas, in our POD meetings, and offsites, that you are make a real direct impact to our success. Please take ~15mins and watch this video.

The second suggestion is Industry Experts Incorporating Security Practices into Business Processes Dr. Tracy Celaya, President and Principal Consultant, Go Consulting International Ira Winkler, President, Secure Mentem https://www.rsaconference.com/events/us18/rsac-ondemand/industry-experts-incorporating-security

there is some really great material. Ira, as always, is fun to listen to and by the time the talk is done you'll feel exhausted.

I hope you take the time, to browse the videos, to see if anything interests you. Keynotes and other videos also available on the rsaconference.com site.

Everyone have an impactful week,
