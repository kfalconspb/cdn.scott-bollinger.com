Editions, Features, Add-Ons, and Kits. Oh My!

Per VMware, there are three editions of vSphere. VMware doesn't call the two kits for smaller deployments, Editions, I don't know why.

Officially, the three editions are vSphere Standard Edition, vSphere Enterprise Edition, vSphere Enterprise Plus Edition. [1 pg 5]

The editions, are bundles of individual features. See the matrix below.

On top of the editions, there are Add-Ons which are bundles of Products.

"VMware vSphere with Operations Management 6 combines two components (vSphere and VMware vRealize™ Operations™ Standard) into a single product with a single license." [1 pg 3]

Next are the Kits

Kits are bundles of features and products grouped together to ease deploy and get things up and running as quickly as possible.
