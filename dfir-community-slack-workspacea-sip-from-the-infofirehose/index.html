DFIR Community Slack Workspace A Sip from the infoFireHose

A free and open community of DFIR professionals

Today's Sip from the infoFireHose is a Slack Workspace focused on digital forensics and incident response.

It's hosted by Demisto, though no vendor products are advertised on the Team channels.

There are approximately 2500 users between the various channels.

The invite page states the purpose as "This community hosts discussions about forensics tools, incident response best practices and playbooks."
