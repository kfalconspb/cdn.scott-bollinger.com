HTML details Tag

A Thing I Learned Today

Posted by Scott Bollinger / @kfalconspb on June 19, 2019 #Development

Needed An Easy QA Solution

I was looking for a native HTML5 way to make a simple Question and Answer solution for little quiz/reflection sections in my blog posts.

I came across many CSS/JS solutions. The simplest solution was to use the details tag. I could pose a questions, unveil an answer, just what I needed.

The details tag encapsulates a summary, and paragraph tag, that when the summary item is clicked, it reveals the paragraph text. There is also a attribute which specifies if the paragraph details are visible.
